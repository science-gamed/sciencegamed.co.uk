Doctor, the fate of the hospital is in your hands...

Inspired by Papers, Please, S.O.R.S is a narrative driven game where you must diagnose and help patients whilst also trying to work out who to trust, and what's going on at the hospital. All this whilst dealing with hackers...

Game features:

  • 6 different endings based on player choice
  • Depending on player choice, gameplay varies and different challenges are experienced
  • Chat to patients to reduce complaints
  • Inspired by real science. Information in the game is based on real disease/treatment information

System requirements:

OS: Windows Vista or later / Mac OS X 10.6 or later

Processor: Intel Dual Core 1.8GHz

Memory: 4 GB RAM

Hard Drive: 1 GB available space

Are you an educator?

SORS is a game for everyone, but is based on real science. It contains themes and topics that are suited to the classroom. We've produced an education guide to help teachers integrate the game into relevant classroom topics.
